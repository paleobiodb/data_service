The Fossil Record:
Taxonomy Data Entry Tip Sheet

Fields:

  • 1. Higher order taxon - Either the senior synonym, taxon being assigned to, or the values "nomen dubium","nomen vanum","nomen nudum", or "nomen oblitum"
  • 2. Lower order taxon - Name of taxon being synonynized, classified, or invalidated.
  • 3. First appearance - Stratigraphic interval from scale entered in the system. See list of scales.
  • 4. Last appearance - Same.
  • 5. Author(s) - Optional. A comma separated list of the authors who named the taxon. You may leave this field blank the database will attempt to autofill if the author is in the sytem.
  • 6. Publication year - Optional. Year taxon was named. You may leave this field blank and the database will attempt to autofill if the author is in the system.
  • 7. Phylogenetic status - Optional. May be [monophyletic, paraphyletic, polyphyletic].
  • 8. Habitat - Optional. May be [terrestrial, marine, freshwater].
  • 9. First occurrence - Example: B. sanpauloensis Shikama & Ozaki, 1966, Irati F., Passa Dois Group, Parana Basin, Brazil
  • 10. Last occurrence
  • 11. Comments -
