Occurrence table tip sheet


  • The abundance unit of all occurrences will be changed to reflect the value selected in the "Abund. unit" pulldown. Thus, all occurrences on the tip sheet must have the same abundance unit, or have no abundance data.

  • If no abundance data is given, a taxon's presense may be denoted with an "x".

  • Abundances denoted "category" or "rank" may be autofilled if you hit the TAB key, if similar values already exist in the form. For example, if you fill in "Equus sp." as "rare" for the first entry, you can enter "r" and hit tab for the second entry to autofill to "rare".
