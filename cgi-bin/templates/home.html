Research in progress

The group has published a collaborative manuscript on Phanerozoic diversity. The vert paleo group is working on a large 5% set of references. Progress on the 5% project is summarized on a separate page. The marine invertebrate group is working on a similar 1% set of references. We have a general-purpose data entry protocol page for the 5% and 1% projects.
