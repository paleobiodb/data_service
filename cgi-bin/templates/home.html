E-mail discussion lists

Please send an e-mail message directly to the paleodb@nceas e-mail list if you would like to discuss issues of general interest to the entire database. Otherwise, you may write to any of the following e-mail lists that we maintain for individual working groups:
  • enterers@nceas.ucsb.edu (data enterers)
  • paleobotany@nceas.ucsb.edu (paleobotany group)
  • pbdbab@nceas.ucsb.edu (Advisory Board)
  • pmpd@nceas.ucsb.edu (Phanerozoic marine diversity group)
  • taphonomy@nceas.ucsb.edu (taphonomy group)
  • vertpaleo@nceas.ucsb.edu (vertebrate paleontology group)
