Paleobiology Database Diversity Curve Request Form

This form allows you to request a diversity curve with assorted statistics.

Note: I plan to add more options for excluding collections that are missing data in any of a variety of categories.

The basic time scale used by this form derives from Harland et al. 1989. Analyses may employ either epochs from the Harland scale or 10 m.y.-long "working intervals" defined by the group.
