Don't abbreviate the name, and be careful to spell it correctly!
Enter higher-ranked taxa first, e.g., enter a genus before its species; enter a family before its genera.
Don't enter the genus name if the species originally was combined in a subgenus.
Example: enter "Scalites tropidophora" instead of "Pleurotomaria (Scalites) tropidophora."
