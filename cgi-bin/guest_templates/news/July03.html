New script and database structure features

  • Collections form revised to include museum repository pulldowns and numerous new fields
  • "PACED" added to lists of research projects and groups
  • Major system upgrade: eight time interval fields in collections table consolidated into just two fields (max and min); three new tables added to handle time scales, interval names, and correlations; two new Perl modules added to handle entry and editing of time scales and "smart" hierarchical searches of collections data using max/min time terms; and collections search, download, map, and tabular report scripts revised to employ the new search method
