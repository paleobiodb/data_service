New script and database structure features

  • New tool that allows fixing large numbers of spelling errors at once
  • New tool that allows quickly finding collections with abundance data and analyzing them with ecological statistics
  • Downloads may exclude form taxa and ichnofossils
  • Heavily revised search form for time scales
  • Additional time scales from:
    • Agusti et al. 2001 (Neogene mammal zones of Europe)
    • Cooper et al. 2004 (Phanerozoic epochs and stages of New Zealand)
    • Heckel and Clayton 2006 (Carboniferous epochs, stages, and substages)
    • Meng and McKenna 1998 (Paleogene mammal ages of Asia)
