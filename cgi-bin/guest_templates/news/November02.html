Paleobiology Database News for November, 2002

Data entered this month

126 references, 509 collections, and 5883 occurrences

New Database people

  • Adam Tomasovych (enterer, Universität Würzburg)

New script and database structure features

  • "diatomaceous" and "gypsiferous" added to lithology adjectives
  • Major revision of map generator to allow paleogeographic reconstructions; a colored continental crust layer; centering of the map on any arbitrarily defined lat/long coordinate; Eckert, Mollweide, and orthographic projections; international and U.S. state borders; up to four sets of data points at once; data points plotted as crosses, diamonds, stars, or triangles in addition to circles or squares; downloads of maps in Adobe Illustrator, GIF, JPEG, or PICT format; multiple new colors; etc.
