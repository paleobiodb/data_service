Major individual data sets reposited in the Paleobiology Database

Data sets are listed alphabetically by authorizer.
  • North American Mammalian Paleofaunal Database 2002
    • Authorizer: John Alroy
    • Enterer: John Alroy
    • Primary reference: J. Alroy. 1996. Constant extinction, constrained diversification, and uncoordinated stasis in North American mammals. Palaeogeography, Palaeoclimatology, Palaeoecology 127(1/4):285-311.
    • To download the data: Search for authorizer = J. Alroy and period = Cretaceous or Tertiary and research group = vertebrate
  • Middle Devonian fossils of the Michigan Basin
    • Authorizer: Brian Bodenbender
    • Enterer: Erin Wysocki
    • Primary reference: C. A. Ferguson, B. E. Bodenbender, J. L. Jones, and K. Ahmed. 2000. Recording the fossil record: a GIS database of Middle Devonian fossils in the Michigan Basin. Geological Society of America, Abstracts with Programs 32(7):A131.
    • To download the data: Search for authorizer = B. Bodenbender and period = Devonian
  • Jurassic marine faunas of France, Greenland, Portugal, and the United Kingdom
    • Authorizer: Franz Fürsich
    • Enterers: Hans Eichiner, Franz Fürsich, Danica Hempfling, Elmar Link
    • To download the data: Search for authorizer = F. Fursich and period = Jurassic
  • Silurian and Early Devonian plants 2001
    • Authorizer: Pat Gensel
    • Enterers: Michele Kotyk, Allison Tuell
    • To download the data: Search for authorizer = P. Gensel and period = Silurian or Devonian
  • Ivany Thesis Collection: Middle Eocene US Gulf Coast Macrofossils
    • Authorizer: Linda Ivany
    • Enterer: Linda Ivany
    • To download the data: Search for authorizer = L. Ivany and epoch = Eocene
  • Maastrichtian bivalve faunas of the world 1994
    • Authorizer: David Jablonski
    • Enterers: John Alroy, David Jablonski
    • Primary reference: D. M. Raup and D. Jablonski. 1993. Geography of end-Cretaceous marine bivalve extinctions. Science 260:971-973.
    • To download the data: Search for authorizer = D. Jablonski and period = Cretaceous
  • Upper Cretaceous larger invertebrate fossils from the Haustator bilira zone of the Atlantic and Gulf Coastal Plains
    • Authorizer: Matt Kosnik
    • Enterer: Matt Kosnik
    • Primary reference: M. A. Kosnik and R. Lupia. 1999. Sampling middle Maastrichtian marine molluscs: spatial and temporal variation. Geological Society of America, Abstracts with Programs A-399.
    • To download the data: Search for authorizer = M. Kosnik and stage= Maastrichtian
  • Ordovician marine faunas of the world
    • Authorizer: Arnie Miller
    • Enterers: Phil Novack-Gottshall, Rich Krause
    • Primary reference: A. I. Miller. 1997. Dissecting Global Diversity Patterns: examples from the Ordovician Radiation. Annual Review of Ecology and Systematics 28:85-104.
    • To download the data: Search for authorizer = A. Miller and period = Ordovician
  • Pennsylvanian-Permian Marine Benthos of the North American Midcontinent
    • Authorizer: Tom Olszewski
    • Enterers: Melissa Gibson, Katrina Nell, Tom Olszewski
    • Primary reference: T. D. Olszewski and M. E. Patzkowsky. 2001, Measuring recurrence of marine biotic gradients: A case study from the Pennsylvanian-Permian Midcontinent. PALAIOS 16:444-460.
    • To download the data: Search for authorizer = T. Olszewski and period = Carboniferous or Permian
  • Paleozoic marine faunas from the paleocontinent of Laurentia 1992
    • Authorizer: J. John Sepkoski, Jr.
    • Enterer: Mike Sommers
    • Primary reference: J. J. Sepkoski, Jr., and A. I. Miller. 1985. Evolutionary faunas and the distribution of Paleozoic benthic communities in space and time. Pp. 153-190 in J. W. Valentine, Ed., Phanerozoic Diversity Patterns: Profiles in Macroevolution. Princeton University Press, Princeton, New Jersey.
    • To download the data: Search for authorizer = J. Sepkoski
  • Carboniferous terrestrial floras of North America and Europe
    • Authorizer: Hallie Sims
    • Enterer: Heather Lindon, Summer Ostrowski
    • To download the data: Search for authorizer = H. Sims and period = Carboniferous
