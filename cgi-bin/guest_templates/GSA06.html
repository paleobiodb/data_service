2006 Paleobiology Database Workshop

The Paleobiology Database will hold a hands-on workshop on Tuesday, October 24th during the annual Geological Society of America meeting in Philadelphia. The workshop will take place from 7:30 to 9:30 PM at the Loews Philadelphia Hotel on 1200 Market Street, two blocks east of Penn Square and two blocks south of the convention center at 12th Street.

Database Coordinator John Alroy and other experienced Database contributors will be on hand to provide instruction. Topics will include:

  • How to log in and navigate the contributors' area
  • Entering references
  • Entering taxonomic names and opinions
  • Entering collections and occurrences
  • Other data entry tools
  • Query, analysis, and download tools

Everyone is welcome to attend, and all professional scientists are welcome to join the Database and become contributors. We also welcome current members who want to familiarize themselves better with the system.

We will use an LCD projector to show you around the web site, and we will enter data together onto a backup server to demonstrate the system. You are welcome to bring your laptop and a published paper of your own choice so you can enter data from it.

Please spread the word about the workshop. We look forward to seeing you there!
