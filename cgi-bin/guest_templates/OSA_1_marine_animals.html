Compendia of Fossil Marine Animal Families and Genera
Online Systematics Archive 1

Scope

This is a Paleobiology Database Online Systematics Archive that lists all Phanerozoic marine animal families and genera. Families and genera are both assigned directly to orders, so genera are not assigned to families.

Scientific goals

The data have been used in numerous publications by many authors having to do with Phanerozoic diversity and exinction patterns.

History

The data were compiled by Jack Sepkoski through 1999. The data were uploaded in January, 2003.

Publications

  • J. J. Sepkoski, Jr. 1982. A compendium of fossil marine families. Milwaukee Public Museum Contributions in Biology and Geology 51:1-125.
  • J. J. Sepkoski, Jr. 1992. A compendium of fossil marine animal families, 2nd edition. Milwaukee Public Museum Contributions in Biology and Geology 83:1-156.
  • J. J. Sepkoski, Jr. 2002. A compendium of fossil marine animal genera. Bulletins of American Paleontology 363:1-560.
