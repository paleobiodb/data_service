Terms of use
If you intend to use the data in a publication, we ask you to:

  • Notify some of the major contributors about your research.
  • Invite them to join your research project if you think it would be appropriate.
  • Either cite the download, acknowledge the major contributors by name in your paper, or cite the relevant Online Systematics Archive.
  • Join the PaleoDB and contribute more data.
  • At a bare minimum, request a publication number once your paper is accepted.

If you agree to these terms, click here to access the data.

How to cite the data

Example using Paleobiology format:

%%paleobiology_authors%% %%year%%. %%title%%. Paleobiology Database. http://paleodb.org.

You can also upload the citation using RIS format.

Example using RIS format:

TY DBASE
AU %%major_contributors%%
%%keywords%% PY %%year%%
TI %%title%%
DB Paleobiology Database
UR http://paleodb.org
Y2 %%year%%
N1 The data file consists of %%occurrence_count%% occurrences that were downloaded on %%day%% %%month%% %%year%%. They derive from %%ref_count%% references and were contributed to the Paleobiology Database by %%contributor_count%% researchers.
