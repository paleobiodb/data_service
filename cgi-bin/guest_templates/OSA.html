Paleobiology Database Online Systematics Archives

The Paleobiology Database serves as the repository for a series of major systematics data sets developed by individual participants. The scopes, scientific goals, histories, publications, and contact information related to each archive are listed on the following pages. You can create your own archive of authoritative classification data by joining the Database.

  • Compendia of Fossil Marine Animal Families and Genera by Jack Sepkoski

  • Gulf Coastal Plain Molluscs by Matthew Kosnik

  • North American Fossil Mammal Systematics Database by John Alroy

  • Paleozoic Gastropod, Rostroconch and "Monoplacophoran" (Tergomyan and Helcionelloid) Database by Peter Wagner

  • Taxonomy and Classification of Non-Avian Dinosauria by Matthew Carrano

  • Tetrapod Classification by Robert Carroll
